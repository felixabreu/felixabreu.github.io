  • What Inspired Sofit?

    After not being happy with how he looked, Steven Ogidi (Founder of SoFit) decided to go on a journey to transform his life and face one of the toughest challenges he'd face yet, obesity. Despite not having many resources, in a matter of six months Steven went from 310 pounds to 190, following diet and workout plans he'd created for himself. Having witnessed his own achievement, he decided to create SoFit with the goal of helping others by equipping them with the necessary resources to achieve their personal transformations.
